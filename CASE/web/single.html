An Informal Residency - A Photography Exhibition Fundraiser

Initiated By Brittany Hake    on June 2, 2016    Comments (3)

In less than four months I have my 1st photography exhibition. It's a bit scary, a bit out there, especially since I am a nurse. I've tried to be a photographer for years. I've learnt so much, created so many pictures, given a lot away, but I do it for the joy, the passion and because to me photography is like breathing. I have so many ideas and limited time to do them so photography has always been my spare time. For my exhibition I have so many pictures to chose from. I've booked the place, overlooking the Gold Coast coastline, I have the date locked in, Monday 6th February. But there is so much more to do and for that I'm wanting to raise funds. I need to print my works, organise the exhibition, have catering, advertising and the time to do it all.
